Team Nigma fan page

About

Team Nigma is a European esports organization founded by former Team Liquid members and four out of five of the Champions of The International 2017. After finishing 2nd at The International 2019, captain KuroKy announced the team's departure from Team Liquid with the idea of forming their own organization.
